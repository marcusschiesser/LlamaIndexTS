Vectorstores

Vectorstores lets you build retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) apps by indexing documents as embeddings and retrieving the most relevant chunks at query time.

What you can do

  • Load documents from files like PDF, DOCX, CSV, Markdown, and more.
  • Split documents into chunks for better retrieval.
  • Create embeddings and store them in an index.
  • Retrieve relevant chunks to answer questions.
